Mission

Pipe Internet exists to abundantly improve the information infrastructure of Toledo and Northwest Ohio for all residents and businesses. We seek to do this by offering order of magnitude faster Internet service than what is currently available, along with affordable pricing. We hope to reach people of all walks of life and positively alter the course of technology in Toledo while spuring innovation.
